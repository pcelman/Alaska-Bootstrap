Alaskan Odyssey: A Family Constellations Cruise

Embark on a journey that combines natural beauty and personal growth on this Alaska cruise. Not only will you be surrounded by stunning landscapes, but you will also have the chance to deepen your understanding of family constellations with a lineup of expert facilitators. Get ready for a trip that promises to awaken your senses and inspire deeper perspectives.

Meet the

facilitators

In memory of Bert Hellinger

Bert Hellinger was a renowned psychologist and therapist who left an indelible impact on the world through his pioneering work in the field of family constellations. Hellinger passed away, but his teachings continue to live on and influence countless individuals around the world.

Brigitte Champetier de Ribes

Brigitte studied psychology in Paris and is a licensed psychologist and psychotherapist. In 2005, she opened the Institute of Family Constellations in Madrid, where she offers workshops and training with a focus on constellations. In 2008 and 2010, she organized seminars in Madrid led by Bert Hellinger.

Joan Garriga

Humanistic Psychologist and Gestalt Therapist who introduced Bert Hellinger in Spain.
Imparts training and workshop in Family Constellations throughout Spain and Latin America. Co-founder of the Gestalt Institute of Barcelona.
Writer.

Thomas Hübl

Thomas Hübl, PhD, is a renowned teacher, author, and international facilitator whose lifelong work integrates the core insights of the great wisdom traditions and mysticism with the discoveries of science. Since 2004, he has taught and facilitated programs with more than 100,000 people worldwide.

Alaska - October 2023

A cruise through Alaska promises to be a breathtaking experience filled with natural wonders and a chance to connect with others. This particular cruise offers an added bonus with a lineup of speakers who will be discussing the topic of family constellations. During this journey, passengers will have the opportunity to take part of the workshops.
The breathtaking scenery of Alaska serves as the perfect backdrop for a thought-provoking and enlightening experience. So pack your bags and get ready for a cruise that promises to be both scenic and educational.
